Philippine Native Animals and Places

The archipelago's diverse geography, from limestone karst mountains to ancient coral reefs supports this high level of biodiversity. Many of the country's most famous species are concentrated in specific islands and conservation centers.

The Philippines is home to a wide variety of native animals, including the Philippine eagle, tarsier, and carabao. The country also boasts stunning natural landscapes such as the Chocolate Hills, Banaue Rice Terraces, and Taal Volcano. These unique animals and places contribute to the rich biodiversity and cultural heritage of the Philippines, making it a fascinating destination for nature lovers and travelers alike.

Mount Purro Nature-reserve

Mount Purro Nature Reserve is a protected area located in the Philippines, known for its rich biodiversity and conservation efforts. It serves as a sanctuary for various native species, including the Philippine eagle, and offers opportunities for eco-tourism and environmental education.
