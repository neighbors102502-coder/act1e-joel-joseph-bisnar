Travel - Hike the peaks of beauty and peace

Explore the majestic mountains of the Philippines, known for their lush greenery, diverse wildlife, and breathtaking views. Popular destinations like Mount Apo and mount Pulag offer thrilling adventures and a peaceful retreat for nature lovers. Hikers can enjoy trekking, bird watching, and camping under the stars.

Mount Pulag - Benguet

Mount Pulag, located in Benguet, Philippines. is the third highest mountain in the Philippines and is known for its stunning sunrise views, sea of clouds, and unique plants and trees. It is a popular destination for hikers and nature enthusiasts seeking a challenging yet rewarding trekking experience.

The entire hike up and back down the mountain can take 5-6 hours, and the route though easy in most parts, can get steep in a few areas. Also, the temperatures on the trail could drop to zero or below. The total length of the trail is almost 17.4km.

Mount Apo - Mindanao

Mount Apo, located in Mindanao, Philippines is the highest mountain in the Philippines, standing at 2,954 meters above sea level. If you plan to undertake the hike, Kapatagan is the nearest town to reach. Several trails are found across the mountain of varying difficulty.

The mountain is well known for the thick moss that covers up the trails. But of course, the trekkers are rewarded with amazing views of the lakes, countryside, and sky.

Mount Batulao - Zambales

Mount Batulao is a popular hike because it is ideal for beginners. Located in Zambales, Philippines, it is about 3 hours from Manila; the rolling slopes and scenic hiking routes are inviting. Also, one crosses several mini-peaks before reaching the actual summit. The trek may take around 4 hours, but there are campsites to rest and spend the night if required.
