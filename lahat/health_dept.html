Department of Health

The Department of Health (DOH) is the government agency in the Philippines in charge of public health. It makes health policies and manages programs to prevent and control diseases. It also works to provide quality and accessible healthcare for all Filipinos.

🏬

Department of Interior and Local Government

The DILG is a Philippine government agency that manages local governments. It ensures peace, order, and public safety. It also oversees the police and fire services.

🏦

Department of Social Welfare and Development

The DSWD is a Philippine government agency that helps protect and support people in need. It runs programs for poverty aid, livelihood, and disaster relief. It is currently led by Secretary Rex Gatchalian.

DOH - Department of Health

The Department of Health (DOH) is the principal executive department of the Philippine government responsible for ensuring equitable, accessible, and quality health services for all Filipinos. It acts as the nation’s technical authority on health, formulating national policies, regulating health products/services, and managing public health programs to prevent diseases.

DILG - Department of Interior and Local Government

The Department of the Interior and Local Government (DILG) is the Philippine executive department responsible for strengthening local government capability, maintaining peace and order, and ensuring public safety. It supervises local government units (LGUs), oversees the PNP, BFP, and BJMP, and promotes local autonomy.

DSWD - Department of Social Welfare and Development

The Department of Social Welfare and Development (DSWD) is the Philippine government's executive agency responsible for protecting social welfare rights, promoting social development, and providing social assistance. Led by Secretary Rex Gatchalian, it implements key programs like the Pantawid Pamilyang Pilipino Program (4Ps) and Sustainable Livelihood Program (SLP), and provides disaster relief.
