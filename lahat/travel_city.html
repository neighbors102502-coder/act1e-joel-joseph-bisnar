Metro Manila

Metro Manila is the capital of the Philippines and offers many places to visit. Intramuros is a historic area from the Spanish colonial period. You can also visit the National Museum and Rizal Park to learn about Philippine history and culture.

Cebu City

Cebu City is a lively city in the Philippines known for its history, culture, and nightlife. It has famous landmarks like Basilica del Santo Niño, Casa Gorordo Museum, and Magellan’s Cross. These places show the city’s rich heritage and Spanish colonial history.

Davao City

Davao City is a major city in Mindanao and a gateway to Mount Apo, the highest mountain in the Philippines. It is also known for lively spots like Roxas Avenue Night Market and Matina Town Square. These places offer food, shopping, and entertainment for both locals and tourists.

The Capital of the Philippines - Metro Manila

Metro Manila is the capital city of the Philippines. There are a lot of places you can visit here, Intramuros "The Walled City," is the oldest district in Manila. This is what remains of the fortified city, which was the seat of power during Spanish Colonial times (1521-1898). National Museum This grand exhibit house features the country's most historic works of culture and national heritage, including the paintings of heralded Philippine artist Felix Resurreccion Hidalgo. Rizal Park the most famous monument in this park would be of jose rizal , who penned political writings that motivated the philippines revolution, directly across from the monument is the current location for km 0 marker

Queen City of the South - Cebu City

Cebu City, is a vibrant city in the Philippines known for its rich history, beautiful beaches, and lively nightlife. Basilica del Santo Niño, This famous church dates back to the early Spanish colonial era. The Church was founded by the Augustinian friars since their arrival in 1565. You could also find in the church the miraculous image of Sto. Nino. Casa Gorordo Museum, Casa Gorordo is a house museum located in the historic Parian district of Cebu, the oldest city in the Philippines. Built in the 1850's, it lived through several important social changes that shaped modern Filipino identity. Magellan's Cross, This is the spot where Ferdinand Magellan planted a cross in the soil in 1521 and claimed the Philippines for Spain.

Durian capital of the Philippines - Davao City

Davao City, Davao City is a gateway to Mindanos, one of the Philippines' largest islands, and home to Mt. Apo, one of the highest mountains in the archipelago. Roxas Avenue Night Market It's known for its vibrant atmosphere, where locals and tourists alike can enjoy a variety of Filipino street food, local delicacies, and affordable goods in an open-air market setting. Matina Town Square is a vibrant and bustling commercial hub that offers a unique blend of entertainment, dining, and shopping experiences. This popular destination caters to both locals and tourists alike, with its lively atmosphere and diverse range of activities.
