Travel - Escape to the finest coastal paradise

Explore the stunning beaches of the Philippines, known for their crystal-clear waters, soft white sand, and breathtaking sunsets. Popular destinations like Boracay and Palawan offer beautiful views and a relaxing atmosphere for visitors. Travelers can enjoy swimming, snorkeling, island hopping, or simply unwinding by the shore while listening to the waves.

White Beach - Boracay Island

Boracay Island is a small island located in the province of Aklan, Philippines. It is known for its stunning beaches, crystal-clear waters, and vibrant nightlife.

White Beach is one of the best beaches in the Philippines and has always been the star of the show in Boracay Island. Travelers from all over the Philippines and the world can't help but admire this Boracay beach's breathtaking beauty.

Paradise Beach - Bantayan Island

Paradise Beach in Bantayan Island, Cebu is a serene, secluded, and pristine destination featuring fine white sand, clear turquoise waters, and rocky cliffs.

Paradise beach is perfect for relaxing, swimming, and snorkeling. It is a private property with a low entrance fee ranging from PHP 50 to PHP 70.

Free use of small cottages, but there are no restaurants or shops on-site. So Bring your own food and drinks, sunscreen, and proper footwear for the rocky areas.

Malcapuya Island - Coron, Palawan

Malcapuya Island, located in Coron, Palawan, is a renowned tropical destination famous for its powdery white sand beaches and crystal-clear turquoise waters. Often called the "Boracay of Coron," it offers a relaxing, scenic escape featuring palm trees, snorkeling, and vibrant marine life. It is typically visited via boat tours from Coron Town, often as part of the Island Escapade tour.
