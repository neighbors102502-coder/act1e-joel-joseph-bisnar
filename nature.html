Science & Nature in the Philippines

The Philippines is one of only 17 megadiverse countries in the world. From the depths of the Philippine Trench to the peaks of the Cordilleras, our nature is a complex system of biological evolution and tectonic force.

In this section, we go beyond the surface to explore the Geomorphology, Meteorology, and Ecology that make our islands a unique scientific frontier.
